# Email Settings

::: info Free Version
This page covers the Free Version. [View Pro docs →](/settings/email)
:::

The **Email Settings** section is where you configure the sender and reply-to information for every email that Zeno Chatbot sends out. These settings determine what your visitors see in their inbox when they receive an email from your chatbot — from conversation summaries to support follow-ups.

## Accessing Email Settings

![Email settings page](/images/free/page48_1.png)

Click the **Settings** tab in the top-right corner of your Zeno Chatbot page. From the left-hand settings menu, click **Email Settings**. This is the fifth tab in the list.

## Configuring Email Settings

### From Name

![From name field](/images/free/page49_1.png)

The name that appears in the **From** field of outbound emails. This is usually your brand or website name — for example, *"Cookieshop"* or *"Zeno Support"*. Visitors will see this name when your email lands in their inbox, so pick something recognizable.

### From Email

![From email field](/images/free/page49_2.png)

The email address used in the **From** field of outbound emails. This is the address that emails will appear to come from. For best deliverability, use an address on your own domain (for example, `support@yoursite.com`) rather than a free email provider.

### Reply-To Email

![Reply-to email field](/images/free/page50_1.png)

The email address where replies from your visitors will be sent. If a recipient hits "Reply" to an email from Zeno, their message will go to this address. This field is optional — if left empty, replies will default to the **From Email** address.

### Reply-To Name

![Reply-to name field](/images/free/page50_2.png)

The name that appears in the **Reply-To** field. This is optional and is used alongside the Reply-To Email. A common example is *"Support Team"*. If you don't set this, only the Reply-To Email address will be shown on replies.

## Saving Your Settings

![Save settings](/images/free/page51_1.png)

Once you have configured your From and Reply-To details, click the **Save Settings** button at the bottom-right of the page to apply your changes.

Your Email Settings are now configured, and every email sent by Zeno Chatbot will use these details going forward.
