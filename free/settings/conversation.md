# Conversation Settings

::: info Free Version
This page covers the Free Version. [View Pro docs →](/settings/conversation)
:::

The **Conversation Settings** section is where you control how Zeno Chatbot manages conversations once they are no longer active. From automatically closing stale chats to generating summaries after they end, these settings help keep your conversation log clean and give you quick insights into every chat.

## Accessing Conversation Settings

![Conversation settings page](/images/free/page45_1.png)

Click the **Settings** tab in the top-right corner of your Zeno Chatbot page. From the left-hand settings menu, click **Conversation Settings**. This is the fourth tab in the list.

## Configuring Conversation Settings

### Auto Close Conversations

![Auto close toggle](/images/free/page46_1.png)

Use this toggle to automatically close inactive open conversations after a configured time window. When **enabled**, any conversation that goes quiet for the specified amount of time will be marked as closed on its own — without you needing to do it manually. This is useful for keeping your Conversations dashboard clean and focused on chats that are actually active.

The current status is shown on the right (*Enabled* or *Disabled*). By default, this option is set to **Disabled**.

### Auto Generate Summary On Close

![Auto summary toggle](/images/free/page46_2.png)

Use this toggle to automatically generate a short AI-generated summary of a conversation once it is closed. When **enabled**, every closed conversation will include a quick overview you can read without having to scroll through the full chat history. This is especially helpful when you need to review many conversations at once or track common customer issues.

The current status is shown on the right (*Enabled* or *Disabled*). By default, this option is set to **Enabled**.

## Saving Your Settings

![Save settings](/images/free/page47_1.png)

Once you have configured your auto-close and auto-summary preferences, click the **Save Settings** button at the bottom-right of the page to apply your changes.

Your Conversation Settings are now configured, and Zeno will handle closed chats according to your preferences from here on.
