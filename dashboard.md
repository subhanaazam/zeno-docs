# Dashboard Overview

Zeno Chatbot makes it easy to monitor all your chatbot activity right from your WordPress dashboard. As the admin, you'll get a clear and simple view of how your chatbot is performing.

From the dashboard, you'll see key stats like total conversations, resolved chats, average response time, token usage, and more — all in one place.

![Dashboard overview](/images/page3_1.png)

## Key Performance Metrics

At the top of the dashboard, you'll find a series of boxes showing your most important stats:

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Total Conversations** | The total number of chat sessions your chatbot has handled since it was activated. |
| **Resolved** | The total number of conversations successfully resolved, along with how many were resolved today. |
| **Average Response Time** | The average time your chatbot takes to reply. Shown as *N/A* until enough data is collected. |

![Resolved metric and Average Response Time](/images/page4_1.png)
![Resolved metric detail](/images/page4_2.png)
| **Token Usage** | The total number of tokens consumed by your chatbot across all conversations. Helps you track and control AI costs. |

![Token Usage metric](/images/page5_1.png)

## Top Navigation Tabs

In the top-right corner of your Zeno Chatbot page, you'll find three main navigation tabs:

- **Dashboard** — the main overview screen
- **Conversations** — opens the full conversations log where you can browse and manage every chat
- **Settings** — opens the full settings panel where you can configure your chatbot's behavior, appearance, and data sources

To the right of Settings, you'll also see a **help icon** and a **gift icon** for quick access to support and promotional content.

![Navigation tabs with help and gift icons](/images/page6_1.png)

## Performance Graph

Below the main stats, a graph gives you a visual representation of your chatbot's performance over time.

You can switch between two views using the tabs at the top of the graph:

- **Conversations** — shows how many conversations occurred each day
- **Token Usage** — shows how many tokens were consumed each day

Use the **date range filter** at the top of the graph to view data for specific periods, such as the last 30 days or a custom range.

![Performance graph - conversations view](/images/page6_2.png)
![Performance graph - token usage view](/images/page7_1.png)

## Right Sidebar

The sidebar on the right gives you a real-time snapshot of recent activity.

- **Recent Conversations** — this list shows the most recent chats. You can quickly see who the user was, when the chat happened, and its status. If there are no recent chats, you'll see a "No recent conversations" message.
- **View All Conversations** — click this link at the bottom of the sidebar to jump straight to the full Conversations page where you can browse every chat in categories of Active, Closed, and All.
