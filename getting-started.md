# What is Zeno Chatbot?

Welcome to **Zeno Chatbot** — your all-in-one AI-powered chatbot solution for building, managing, and deploying intelligent conversations right inside your WordPress site.

Whether you're an eCommerce store owner, a SaaS company, or a content creator, Zeno Chatbot gives you the tools to turn your website visitors into engaged customers with AI-driven conversations, with **zero coding required**.

## Why Use Zeno Chatbot?

Imagine an intelligent assistant that answers your customers' questions 24/7, guides them to the right products, and handles support tickets automatically — all from your WordPress dashboard.

Here's what Zeno brings:

- **Launch your own AI chatbot** in minutes — no 3rd-party platform needed
- **Connect your preferred LLM provider** like OpenAI or Google Gemini
- **Train the chatbot** using your own website content, sitemaps, and products
- **Customize the chat widget** to match your brand — colors, avatar, greeting, and more
- **Manage every conversation** from a centralized dashboard
- **Set up saved replies** for common questions to save tokens and ensure accuracy
- **Track performance** with real-time analytics — conversations, response times, and token usage

## Who Is Zeno Chatbot For?

Zeno Chatbot is perfect for:

- **Store owners using WooCommerce** who want to automate product support
- **SaaS and service businesses** that want to reduce support tickets
- **Bloggers and content creators** looking to engage readers with smart Q&A
- **Anyone** who wants to offer 24/7 support without hiring a full team

No matter what your website is about, if you want to provide intelligent, instant answers to your visitors, Zeno Chatbot has you covered.

## How Does It Work?

Here's a quick look at the flow:

1. **Install Zeno Chatbot** — follow the installation guide
2. **Connect your preferred AI provider** — add your API key in the LLM settings
3. **Build a knowledge index** from your website content, sitemap, or products
4. **Customize the chat widget** with your branding, greeting, and behavior
5. **Visitors start chatting** and Zeno answers using your content and the selected LLM
6. **Review conversations, track tokens, and refine replies** — all from your dashboard

It's simple, scalable, and built to grow with your website.
