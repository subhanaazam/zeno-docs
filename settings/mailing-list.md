# Mailing List

::: info Pro Feature
Mailing List is available in the **Pro version** only. [View Free docs →](/free/getting-started)
:::

The **Mailing List** section is where Zeno Chatbot stores and manages the contact information of users who have interacted with your chatbot. This gives you a central place to view, search, import, and export your chatbot's contact list — making it easy to follow up with leads or sync contacts with your email marketing tools.

## Accessing Mailing List

Click the **Settings** tab in the top-right corner of your Zeno Chatbot page. From the left-hand settings menu, click **Mailing List**.

## Mailing List Overview

![Mailing List overview](/images/pro2/page12_1.png)

At the top of the Mailing List page, a **contact count** shows how many contacts are currently in your list (for example, *"0 contacts total"*).

## Searching Contacts

Use the **search bar** at the top of the contact table to find a specific contact by typing their name or email address.

## Importing and Exporting Contacts

![Import and export buttons](/images/pro2/page13_1.png)

Two action buttons appear at the top-right of the Mailing List page:

- **Import CSV** — Click this button to import a list of contacts from a CSV file. This is useful if you want to bring in an existing email list or migrate contacts from another tool.
- **Export CSV** — Click this button to download your entire mailing list as a CSV file. This is useful for backing up your contacts or importing them into an external email marketing platform.

## Contacts Table

![Contacts table](/images/pro2/page13_2.png)

The main table lists every contact in your mailing list. The columns include:

![Contacts table columns](/images/pro2/page14_1.png)

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Name** | The contact's full name |
| **Email** | The contact's email address |
| **Type** | How the contact was added — for example, automatically captured through a chat conversation or manually imported |
| **Added** | The date the contact was added to the list |

If no contacts exist yet, the table will show a **"No Data"** message.

::: tip
Use **Export CSV** regularly to back up your mailing list and sync it with your email marketing platform (Mailchimp, ConvertKit, etc.). Keeping an external copy ensures you don't lose contact data.
:::
