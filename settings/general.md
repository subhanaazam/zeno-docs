# General Settings

The **General Settings** section is where you configure the core behavior and personality of your Zeno Chatbot. This is the first place you should visit after installing the plugin, as these settings define how your chatbot communicates with your visitors in every conversation.

## Accessing General Settings

Click the **Settings** tab in the top-right corner of your Zeno Chatbot page. From the left-hand settings menu, click **General Settings**. This is the first tab in the list and is selected by default when you open the settings panel.

## Language Settings

![Language Settings dropdown](/images/page11_1.png)

Select the primary language for your chatbot from the dropdown menu. This determines the language in which your chatbot will communicate with visitors. By default, this is set to **English (US)**. You can change it at any time to match your target audience.

::: tip
Choose a language that matches the majority of your website visitors for the best user experience.
:::

## System Prompts

![System Prompts field](/images/page12_1.png)

This field is where you define the system instructions and behavior for the AI assistant. It sets the **context and personality** of your chatbot — essentially telling the AI who it is, how it should respond, and what tone it should use.

**Default system prompt:**

```
You are a helpful AI assistant. Provide accurate, professional, and friendly responses
based on the available context. Be concise and clear in your explanations.
```

You can customize this to better match your brand. For example, if you run an online store:

```
You are a friendly customer support assistant for [Your Store Name]. Help customers with
product questions, shipping information, and order inquiries. Always be warm, helpful,
and concise.
```

::: tip
The more specific you are in your system prompt, the better your chatbot will match your brand voice.
:::

## On Error Response

![On Error Response field and Save Settings button](/images/page13_1.png)

This field lets you set the message displayed to users when the AI encounters an error or cannot process a request. This ensures users receive a helpful response even when something goes wrong — rather than leaving them with a silent failure.

**Default error response:**

```
I apologize, but I encountered an issue processing your request. Please try again or
contact our support team if the problem persists.
```

You can edit this message to match your brand tone or to guide users toward a specific action, such as submitting a support ticket or trying again later.

## Saving Your Settings

Once you have configured your language, system prompts, and error response, click the **Save Settings** button at the bottom-right of the page to apply your changes.

Your General Settings are now configured, and your chatbot will use them for every conversation going forward.
