# Knowledge Notes

The **Knowledge Notes** tab lets you add custom written notes directly to your chatbot's knowledge base — without needing to publish them as pages, posts, or PDFs. This is perfect for facts, policies, FAQs, or internal knowledge that you want your chatbot to know but don't want to display publicly on your site.

## Accessing Knowledge Notes

Go to **Settings > Data Source** and click the **Knowledge Notes** tab.

## Adding Knowledge Notes

![Knowledge Notes editor and preview panels](/images/page72_1.png)

The tab is split into two panels side by side: an **Editor** on the left and a **Preview** on the right.

### Editor

Type your notes in the left-hand text area. You can use either **markdown** formatting or **plain text**, whichever you prefer. The placeholder inside the editor reminds you: *"Type markdown or plain text here..."* and a helper note confirms *"Markdown and plain text are supported."*

::: tip
Use headings, lists, and bold text in markdown to structure your notes clearly — this helps the AI understand and retrieve the right information when answering questions.
:::

### Preview

![Knowledge Notes live preview panel](/images/page73_1.png)

The right-hand panel shows a live rendered preview of your notes. If you're writing in markdown, this is where you'll see how the formatting looks once it's rendered. This is purely for your reference as you write.

## Managing Your Notes

![Knowledge Notes action buttons - Clear and Build Index](/images/page74_1.png)

At the top-right of the Knowledge Notes tab, you'll find two action buttons:

| Button | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Build Index** | Save your notes and add them to the chatbot's knowledge base. Your chatbot will then be able to use these notes when answering questions. |
| **Clear** | Remove all of your Knowledge Notes and start fresh. |

You now have a lightweight way to teach your chatbot anything — no new pages, no uploads, just notes.
