# Data Source

The **Data Source** section is where you build the knowledge base your chatbot draws from when answering questions. Instead of relying only on the AI model's general knowledge, Zeno Chatbot can pull from your own website content, external URLs, uploaded files, and custom notes — so answers stay accurate, on-brand, and specific to your business.

## Accessing Data Source

Click the **Settings** tab in the top-right corner of your Zeno Chatbot page. From the left-hand settings menu, click **Data Source**. This is the seventh and final tab in the list.

## Available Knowledge Sources

![Data Source overview with five knowledge source tabs](/images/page60_1.png)

At the top of the Data Source page, you'll find five tabs representing different types of knowledge sources:

| Tab | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| [**Internal Content**](./internal-content) | Index your own WordPress posts, pages, sitemap, and WooCommerce products as a knowledge source. |
| [**External Sources**](./external-sources) | Add external URLs (such as documentation sites or help centers) to expand your chatbot's knowledge beyond your own site. |
| [**Resources**](./resources) | Upload images or PDFs to provide additional context to your AI chatbot. |
| [**Knowledge Notes**](./knowledge-notes) | Add custom markdown or plain text notes directly as AI context — useful for facts that aren't published anywhere on your site. |
| **Automatic Builder** *(Upcoming)* | An automated knowledge base builder coming in a future release. |

Each tab is covered in detail on its own page. Click any of the links above to learn more about that specific knowledge source.
